Computer Lab

Our computer lab provides resources for students to engage with modern, high-impact domains. Facilities include:

  • Desktop computers with mathematical software
  • High-speed internet access
  • Support for domains like Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Quantitative Finance.
  • Printers and scanning facilities

Research Facilities

We provide a strong research culture with expert supervision and resources for innovation.

  • Research workstations
  • Access to academic journals and e-resources
  • Collaborative spaces for research groups
  • Specialized hardware for computational mathematics
