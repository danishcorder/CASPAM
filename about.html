About CASPAM

The Centre for Advanced Studies in Pure and Applied Mathematics (CASPAM) at Bahauddin Zakariya University, Multan, is designed for ambitious graduates who wish to advance from classical mathematical foundations to modern, high-impact domains. [1]

Our Vision

  • To become a center of excellence in mathematical sciences
  • To produce world-class mathematicians and researchers
  • To contribute to national development through mathematical research and innovation

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education in mathematics
  • Foster research culture and innovation
  • Prepare students for emerging career opportunities
  • Collaborate with industry and academic institutions

Chairperson's Message

"Welcome to CASPAM, where we nurture mathematical minds and prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow. Our commitment to excellence drives us to maintain the highest standards in teaching and research."

Prof. Dr. Imran Javaid
Chairperson, CASPAM
