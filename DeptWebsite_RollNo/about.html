About CASPAM

The Centre for Advanced Studies in Pure and Applied Mathematics (CASPAM) at Bahauddin Zakariya University, Multan is a prestigious academic institution dedicated to excellence in mathematical sciences. Our program is designed for ambitious graduates who wish to advance from classical mathematical foundations to modern, high-impact domains.

Our department offers comprehensive programs in pure and applied mathematics, preparing students for careers in academia, research, and industry. We focus on bridging theoretical mathematics with practical applications in emerging fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Quantitative Finance.

Our Vision

  • To become a center of excellence in mathematical sciences
  • To produce world-class mathematicians and researchers
  • To contribute to national development through mathematical research and innovation

Our Mission

CASPAM envisions to attain heights of excellence through quality academic teaching, learning and research so as to develop individuals, industry and society intellectually, professionally and ethically.

  • Provide quality education in mathematics
  • Foster research culture and innovation
  • Prepare students for emerging career opportunities
  • Collaborate with industry and academic institutions
