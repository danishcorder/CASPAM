Welcome to CASPAM

Welcome to the Centre for Advanced Studies in Pure and Applied Mathematics (CASPAM) at Bahauddin Zakariya University, Multan. Our program is designed for ambitious graduates who wish to advance from classical mathematical foundations to modern, high-impact domains such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Quantitative Finance, and Business Analytics.

Our Mission: CASPAM envisions to attain heights of excellence through academic teaching, learning and research so as to develop individuals, industry and society intellectually, professionally and ethically.

Feel free to explore our website using the navigation menu above. Here you will find comprehensive information about our academic programs, distinguished faculty, research facilities, and how to get in touch with us.
